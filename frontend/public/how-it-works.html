Project methodology

How an agreement becomes a readable report

Nyay follows a rule-bound pipeline. It highlights possible concerns for review, but it does not replace advice from a qualified legal professional.

Analysis steps

01

Read

Extract text from pasted content or a text-based PDF.

02

Protect

Redact common personal identifiers before clause analysis.

03

Classify

Detect whether the document is a rental, loan, employment, service, or general agreement.

04

Check

Compare each clause with the indexed rules for the detected agreement type.

05

Explain

Show risk colors, affected rights, deadlines, costs, and negotiation suggestions.

06

Review

Keep uncertain legal questions clearly marked for professional verification.

Color guide

What the review colors mean

  • GreenNo indexed warning was found.
  • YellowThe clause may be negotiable or needs clarification.
  • RedA high-risk pattern needs careful human review.
