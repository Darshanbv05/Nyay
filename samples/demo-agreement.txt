RESIDENTIAL RENTAL AGREEMENT

1. RENT: The Tenant shall pay monthly rent of INR 25,000 on or before the fifth day of each month.

2. SECURITY DEPOSIT: The Tenant shall pay a security deposit equal to six months of rent before taking possession.

3. ENTRY: The Landlord may enter at any time and without notice to inspect the premises.

4. REPAIRS: The landlord is not responsible for repairs, and the Tenant shall bear all repairs including structural and essential repairs.

5. DEPOSIT RETURN: The deposit shall be forfeited in full if the Tenant leaves before the end of the eleven-month term.

6. UTILITIES: The Tenant will pay electricity and water charges based on metered consumption.

7. NOTICE: Either party may end this agreement by giving thirty days' written notice.
